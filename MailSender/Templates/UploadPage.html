Mass-Mail Dispatcher

Welcome to the Mass-Mail Dispatcher, an efficient tool for sending emails to multiple recipients!

With this application, you can easily send emails to a large list of recipients from a CSV file. Whether you need to reach out to customers, clients, or your community, Mass-Mail Dispatcher simplifies the process.

Here's how it works:
  1. Upload a CSV file containing the email addresses of your recipients.
  2. Compose your email by specifying the subject and content.
  3. Click the "Send Emails" button, and we'll take care of the rest!

Save time and effort by using Mass-Mail Dispatcher for your email campaigns. Whether it's for newsletters, announcements, or personalized messages, we've got you covered.
